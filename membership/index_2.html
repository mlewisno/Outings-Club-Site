Membership in OCOC

Become a member of OCOC, and you’ll enjoy these benefits:

  • Eligibility for all open (OCOC-sponsored) trips. Most weekend trips are paid for entirely by OCOC, while longer trips over fall or spring breaks are usually cheaper than living in Oberlin for the week.

  • Free gear rental from our substantial supply.

  • Access to our library of guidebooks, as well help planning your outdoor adventures from OCOC’s experienced officers.

Become a member by filling out the form, below, and bringing the $10 membership fee to office hours. That’s a one-time fee, so paying once gets you membership for all your time at Oberlin.
